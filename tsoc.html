How TSOC Works

Feb 15 - Mar 30

Application Period

Students submit their proposals for the projects they want to work on. Mentors review and select the best candidates.

April 01 - April 30

Community Bonding

Selected students get to know their mentors, read documentation, and get familiar with the codebase.

May 01 - July 30

Coding Phase

Students spend 12 weeks writing code, building features, and making pull requests to TECH INDRO repositories.

August 15

Final Evaluation

Mentors evaluate the work. Successful students receive certificates, swags, and a stipend!
